# Astro Daily - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 7, 2026**

## Overview
Astro Daily ("we", "our", or "the app") is a ChatGPT application that generates personalized horoscope graphics. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information We Collect
- **Zodiac Sign**: When you request a horoscope, we receive your zodiac sign selection
- **Style Preference**: Your chosen visual style (mystical, gradient, or minimalist)
- **No Personal Data**: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information, email addresses, or user accounts

## How We Use Information
The zodiac sign and style preferences are used solely to:
- Generate your personalized horoscope text via OpenAI's GPT-4 API
- Create custom horoscope graphics via OpenAI's DALL-E API
- We do not store your requests or generated content after delivery

## Third-Party Services
Astro Daily uses:
- **OpenAI API**: To generate horoscope text and images. Subject to [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
- **ChatGPT Platform**: Where the app runs. Subject to [OpenAI's Terms of Use](https://openai.com/terms)

## Data Retention
We do not retain any user data. All requests are processed in real-time and not stored.

## Your Rights
Since we don't collect or store personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete.

## Children's Privacy
Astro Daily does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date.
